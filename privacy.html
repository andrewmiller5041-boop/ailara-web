Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026 · Effective immediately

AILARA is built on a simple principle: your health data is yours. We collect the minimum information needed to personalize your coaching experience and we never sell it.

1. Who We Are

AILARA ("we," "us," or "our") is an AI-powered health and fitness coaching application accessible at ailara.app. AILARA is operated as an independent product.

For privacy questions, contact us at support@ailara.app.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Profile information: name, email address, age, sex, height, weight
  • Health and fitness goals, activity level, dietary preferences, allergies
  • Health conditions you choose to share for personalized guidance
  • Traditional Chinese Medicine constitution type (optional)
  • Chat messages and conversations with your AI coach
  • Workout logs, weight entries, habit tracking, and meal logs
  • Water intake and daily check-in responses (energy, soreness, stress)

Information collected automatically:

  • Payment information is processed by Stripe and never stored by AILARA
  • Basic usage data (which features are used) to improve the product
  • Browser type and device type for compatibility purposes

Information we do NOT collect:

  • We do not collect precise location data
  • We do not collect biometric data from wearables
  • We do not use advertising trackers or sell your data to advertisers

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Personalized coaching: Your profile and health information is sent to Anthropic's Claude AI to generate personalized fitness, nutrition, and wellness guidance
  • Meal and workout planning: Your goals, dietary restrictions, and equipment are used to build custom plans
  • Progress tracking: Workout logs and weight entries are stored locally on your device to show your progress over time
  • Account management: Your email is used to restore access if you contact support
  • Payment processing: Subscription payments are handled by Stripe under their privacy policy
  • Product improvement: Aggregate, anonymized usage data helps us improve AILARA

4. Data Storage and Security

Most of your data — including chat history, workout logs, meal plans, habits, and progress — is stored locally on your device using your browser's localStorage. This means it stays on your device and is not uploaded to our servers.

Your profile information is sent to Anthropic's API to power AI coaching responses. Anthropic processes this data under their own privacy policy and does not use your data to train their models by default.

We use HTTPS encryption for all data in transit. Payment information is processed by Stripe and never touches our servers.

Important: Because data is stored locally, clearing your browser cache or switching devices will remove your stored data. We recommend noting your profile details in case you need to set up again.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Anthropic (Claude AI): Processes your messages and profile to generate coaching responses. Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • Stripe: Handles payment processing for Pro and Elite subscriptions. Stripe Privacy Policy
  • Vercel: Hosts the AILARA application. Vercel Privacy Policy
  • Google Fonts: Provides typography. Google may log font requests per their policy.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information through your profile settings
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data by emailing support@ailara.app
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out: You can stop using AILARA at any time and clear your local data through your browser settings

California residents have additional rights under CCPA. EU/UK residents have rights under GDPR. To exercise any right, contact us at support@ailara.app.

7. Children's Privacy

AILARA is intended for users 18 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. Health Information Disclaimer

AILARA provides general health and fitness information for educational purposes only. This is not medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Health information you share is used solely to personalize general wellness guidance. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider before making significant changes to your diet, exercise routine, or health regimen.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of AILARA after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please reach out:

📧 support@ailara.app
